# Organic Content Categories

Unlike paid ads, organic content builds brand affinity, community, and aesthetic identity. No CTAs, no offers, no urgency copy.

## Categories

### Lifestyle / In-Use Scene
Product shown in natural use context. Person using it, product in its "home" environment.
**Works for:** Instagram feed, Facebook, TikTok
**Example:** Hands shaping clay on the mini wheel, kitchen counter morning scene

### Flat-Lay Composition
Top-down arranged layout. Product + complementary objects on a surface.
**Works for:** Instagram feed, Pinterest
**Example:** Pottery wheel kit laid out with tools, plants, coffee cup on linen

### Behind-the-Scenes / Process
Shows creation, packing, studio life. Builds authenticity.
**Works for:** Instagram stories/reels, TikTok
**Example:** Workshop table mid-project, shipping station, raw materials close-up

### Seasonal / Trending Moment
Ties product to current season, holiday, or cultural moment.
**Works for:** All platforms
**Example:** Cozy fall setup with candle + pottery, summer garden crafting

### Aesthetic Mood Board
Curated color palette / texture composition. Product as part of a visual story.
**Works for:** Instagram feed, Pinterest
**Example:** Earth-tone arrangement: terracotta, dried flowers, linen, ceramic pieces

### "Shelfie" / Styled Product Display
Product on a shelf, desk, or display surface among curated objects.
**Works for:** Instagram feed
**Example:** Bookshelf with pottery pieces, plants, books — product centered

### Morning / Evening Routine
Product integrated into a daily ritual scene.
**Works for:** Instagram feed/stories, TikTok
**Example:** Morning light on desk with coffee, journal, and pottery wheel

### Gift / Occasion Styling
Product as a gift — wrapped, in a gift box, or being given.
**Works for:** Instagram feed, Facebook
**Example:** Unwrapped kit on birthday table, holiday gift flat-lay

### Texture / Detail Close-Up
Macro-style focus on product materials, textures, craft details.
**Works for:** Instagram feed, stories
**Example:** Close-up of clay texture, wheel spinning detail, glaze patterns

### Community / UGC-Style
Mimics user-generated content. Casual, slightly imperfect, relatable.
**Works for:** Instagram feed/stories, TikTok
**Example:** Phone-photo style of finished pottery piece, casual desk setup

## Selection Guidance

- Aim for variety across a generation run (≥2 different categories)
- Weight toward categories matching current observation trends
- Seasonal content gets priority when timely
- UGC-style and lifestyle consistently perform across platforms
- Flat-lay and mood board work best for Instagram feed specifically
